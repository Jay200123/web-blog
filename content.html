    Processor

    The ASUS ROG STRIX G15 is powered by The AMD Ryzen 7 4800H is a Renoir APU designed for larger, more powerful laptops with room for better termals.
    The Ryzen features eight Zen 2 cores running at 2.9 GHz (base clock speed) to 4.2 GHz (Boost).
    The CPU also has 8 MB of L3 cache and a Vega series integrated GPU.
    Thread-doubling SMT tech is enabled in this processor, allowing for up to 16 concurrent processing threads.
